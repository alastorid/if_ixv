This release includes the ix FreeBSD Base Driver for Intel(R) Ethernet Network
Connections.

The ix driver supports devices based on the following controllers:
- All 82599 and 82598EB-based 10 gigabit network connections

NOTE: You can download Ethernet drivers at:
https://downloadcenter.intel.com
